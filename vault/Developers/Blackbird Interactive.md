# Blackbird Interactive

## Overview

Blackbird Interactive is a Canadian video game development studio based in Vancouver, British Columbia. The company was founded in 2007 by a team of experienced industry veterans, including former employees from Relic Entertainment and Electronic Arts [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blackbird_Interactive|Wikipedia]] [[https://blackbirdinteractive.com/|Official Site]] [[https://grokipedia.com/page/Blackbird_Interactive|Grokipedia]]. The studio was established by Rob Cunningham, Yossarian King, and Aaron Kambeitz, who had previously worked on the Homeworld series at Relic Entertainment [[https://ca.linkedin.com/company/blackbird-interactive-inc|LinkedIn]] [[https://bccreates.com/blackbird-interactive/|BC Creates]].

Blackbird Interactive is known for their narrative-driven games with distinctive art styles and high-quality cinematics. The company maintains its own internal CG cinematics department, led by the founder of Pixar Canada [[https://ca.linkedin.com/company/blackbird-interactive-inc|LinkedIn]].

## Development

The studio has grown to become one of Vancouver's notable independent developers, working on both original IP and established franchises. Blackbird's portfolio includes work on the Homeworld series, Hardspace: Shipbreaker, and various other projects [[https://www.gematsu.com/companies/blackbird-interactive|Gematsu]].

The company has faced challenges in recent years, including confirmed layoffs in 2023 after several projects were canceled [[https://www.ign.com/articles/homeworld-3-developer-blackbird-interactive-layoffs-confirmed|IGN]]. Despite these challenges, Blackbird continues to develop games and maintain their reputation for quality production values.

## Legacy

Blackbird Interactive has established itself as a key developer in the real-time strategy and space simulation genres, particularly through their work on the Homeworld franchise. The studio's attention to detail in cinematics and narrative design has earned them recognition in the industry [[https://homeworld.fandom.com/wiki/Blackbird_Interactive|Homeworld Fandom]].
