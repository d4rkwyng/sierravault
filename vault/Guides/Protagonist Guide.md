# Protagonist Guide

<small style="color: gray">Last updated: February 2, 2026</small>

Sierra's adventure games are defined by their memorable protagonists. This guide covers the heroes who appeared in multiple games across Sierra's catalog.

---

## Legendary Heroes

### Roger Wilco (Space Quest)
The galaxy's most unlikely hero—a janitor who repeatedly saves the universe through luck, persistence, and an uncanny ability to survive his own incompetence.

- **1986** — [[1986 - Space Quest - The Sarien Encounter|Space Quest: The Sarien Encounter]]
- **1987** — [[1987 - Space Quest II - Vohaul's Revenge|Space Quest II: Vohaul's Revenge]]
- **1989** — [[1989 - Space Quest III - The Pirates of Pestulon|Space Quest III: The Pirates of Pestulon]]
- **1991** — [[1991 - Space Quest I - Roger Wilco in the Sarien Encounter|Space Quest I VGA]]
- **1991** — [[1991 - Space Quest IV - Roger Wilco and the Time Rippers|Space Quest IV: Roger Wilco and the Time Rippers]]
- **1993** — [[1993 - Space Quest V - The Next Mutation|Space Quest V: The Next Mutation]]
- **1995** — [[1995 - Space Quest 6 - Roger Wilco in the Spinal Frontier|Space Quest 6: Roger Wilco in the Spinal Frontier]]

**Fan Games:** [[2001 - Space Quest - The Lost Chapter|The Lost Chapter]], [[2011 - Space Quest II VGA Remake|SQ2 VGA]], [[2011 - Space Quest - Vohaul Strikes Back|Vohaul Strikes Back]], [[2012 - Space Quest - Incinerations|Incinerations]]
