# Fan & Community Resources

Want to dig deeper than these Markdown notes? These sites preserve interviews, downloads, and discussions across the Sierra fandom.

## General Sierra Hubs
- [SierraHelp.com](https://www.sierrahelp.com/) - Archive of official patches, installers, and troubleshooting guides for classic Sierra games.
- [SierraHelp Forums](https://forums.sierrahelp.com/) - Active discussion board for Sierra tech support, fan games, and preservation.
- [Sierra Chest](https://www.sierrachest.com/) - Comprehensive database with release notes, developer bios, and magazine scans. ([Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/sierrachest))
- [Sierra Gamers](https://www.sierragamers.com/) - Ken Williams' official site preserving Sierra history with game manuals and [forums](https://www.sierragamers.com/forums/).
- [Quest Studios Archive](https://www.queststudios.com/) - Soundtrack rips and MIDI arrangements covering King's Quest through Space Quest.
- [ScummVM Wiki - Sierra](https://wiki.scummvm.org/index.php?title=Sierra) - Technical documentation for AGI/SCI engine compatibility.

## Series-Specific Sites

### King's Quest
- [King's Quest Omnipedia (Fandom)](https://kingsquest.fandom.com/wiki/King's_Quest_Omnipedia) - Comprehensive wiki covering all KQ games, characters, and lore.
- [Daventry Chronicles](https://frodobaggins.wixsite.com/daventrychronicles) - Fan site dedicated to King's Quest history and community.

### Space Quest
- [SpaceQuest.Net](https://spacequest.net/) - Archive for Two Guys content, concept art, and fan game downloads.
- [Space Quest Omnipedia (Fandom)](https://spacequest.fandom.com/wiki/Space_Quest_Omnipedia) - Detailed wiki with timelines, character bios, and fan content.
- [Guys from Andromeda](https://guysfromandromeda.com/) - Official site for Scott Murphy & Mark Crowe's SpaceVenture project.

### Quest for Glory
- [Quest for More Glory (Archive)](https://web.archive.org/web/20240322150642/http://www.questformoreglory.com/) - Strategy guides, hero import tools, and Hero-U updates.

### Leisure Suit Larry
- [Larry Laffer Dot Net](http://larrylaffer.net/) - Premier LSL fan site with forums, behind-the-scenes "Black Book" content, and music archives.
- [Leisure Suit Larry Omnipedia (Fandom)](https://leisuresuitlarry.fandom.com/wiki/Leisure_Suit_Larry) - Wiki covering all LSL games and characters.
- [Al Lowe's Humor Site](http://allowe.com/) - Series creator's official site with [Larry Links](http://allowe.com/games/larry/even-more-larry/larry-links.html), game history, and CyberJoke 3000 newsletter.

### Gabriel Knight
- [Gabriel Knight Fandom Wiki](https://gabrielknight.fandom.com/wiki/Gabriel_Knight_Wiki) - Character biographies, walkthrough links, and canonical timeline debates.

## Forums & Communities
- [Sierra Community Forums](https://www.sierragamers.com/forums/) - Ken Williams' hosted forum for Sierra discussion and fan projects.
- [Reddit r/Sierra](https://www.reddit.com/r/Sierra/) - General Sierra discussion, tech support, and nostalgia.
- [Reddit r/leisuresuitlarry](https://www.reddit.com/r/leisuresuitlarry/) - Active fan community for classic LSL discussion.
- [SierraHelp Forums](https://forums.sierrahelp.com/) - Tech support and fan game discussions.
- [AGDI](https://www.agdinteractive.com/) & [Infamous Adventures](https://www.infamous-adventures.com/) - Fan hubs hosting remakes, voice packs, and developer diaries.

## Interviews & Podcasts
- [Space Quest Historian Podcast](https://spacequesthistorian.com/podcast/) - Bi-weekly interviews with Scott Murphy, Mark Crowe, Josh Mandel, and fan designers. ([Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/space-quest-historian-podcast-by-troels-pleimert-tech/id739423439))
- [Adventure Game Hotspot](https://adventuregamehotspot.com/) - Modern coverage of Sierra-related Kickstarters and retrospectives. ([Podcast](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/our-favorite-sierra-adventure-game-worlds/id1638963659))
- [Scene World Podcast](https://sceneworld.org/) - Retro gaming podcast featuring Sierra creator interviews.

## Developer Studios (Post-Sierra)
- [Cygnus Entertainment](https://cygnusentertainment.com/) - Ken & Roberta Williams' studio behind *Colossal Cave 3D Adventure*. ([History](https://cygnusentertainment.com/history/))
- [Guys from Andromeda](https://guysfromandromeda.com/) - Scott Murphy & Mark Crowe's studio developing SpaceVenture.

## Preservation Projects
- [Archive.org Sierra Collections](https://archive.org/search?query=Sierra%20On-Line) - Scans of InterAction magazine, hint books, and catalog flyers.
- [ScummVM](https://www.scummvm.org/) & [DOSBox](https://www.dosbox.com/) communities - Compatibility notes and nightly builds for running AGI/SCI titles on modern systems.
- [ScummVM Wiki - AGI](https://wiki.scummvm.org/index.php/AGI) - Technical documentation for Sierra's Adventure Game Interpreter.
