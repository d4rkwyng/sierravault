# Sierra Documentary — Legends of Adventure

<small style="color: gray">Last updated: May 13, 2026</small>

## Overview

*Legends of Adventure: The Story of Sierra On-Line* is a feature-length documentary about [[Ken Williams]], [[Roberta Williams]], and the founding-era story of [[Sierra On-Line]] — the company that pioneered the graphical adventure game and trained a generation of designers whose work the vault catalogs. The film is produced by Decently Creative, the team behind the Prime Video docuseries *Gamebreakers*, and is the first feature documentary to give the Williamses an extended on-camera account of their founding-and-departure arc, including why they returned to game development with [[2023 - Colossal Cave 3D Adventure|Colossal Cave 3D Adventure]] in 2023.[^ref-1][^ref-2]

The project ran a successful crowdfunding campaign in July–August 2024 across Kickstarter (initially) and Backerkit, where active community updates have continued through 2026.[^ref-3][^ref-4][^ref-5] As of mid-2026 the film is in post-production, with a complete rough cut and a confirmed 2026 release target.[^ref-6]

This page is a vault-side tracker for the documentary's interviewees, production milestones, and external coverage. Per vault editorial policy, only verified items appear here — dates that could not be confirmed from a primary source are deliberately omitted rather than assumed.

## Subject Matter

The film centers on the **founding and growth of Sierra On-Line** under Ken and Roberta Williams (1979 onward), the **company's mid-1990s commercial peak**, and the Williamses' **20+ year departure from the industry** before their 2020s return with Colossal Cave. The documentary also surveys the broader **rise of the graphical adventure-game genre** that Sierra catalyzed, drawing in voices from across the era.[^ref-1][^ref-2]

Production materials referenced in Backerkit updates include archival photographs, period videos, internal Sierra documents, personal photographs from key Sierra figures, and contemporary news clips originally broadcast on CNN.[^ref-6]
