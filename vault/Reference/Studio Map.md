# Studio Map

<small style="color: gray">Last updated: May 11, 2026</small>

## Overview

This is the comprehensive index of every game-development studio represented in the Sierra extended catalogue — Sierra's own internal studios, the studios Sierra acquired, the partner studios Sierra funded and published, alumni-founded studios that continued the Sierra design tradition, and the fan-developer studios that have produced remakes and spiritual successors. Each entry summarizes founding/closure, current status, parent company, location, and the studios' Sierra-era and post-Sierra credits.

Used together with [[Corporate Lineage|Corporate Lineage]] and the per-studio Developer pages, this gives a single-page answer to questions like "Which studios contributed to the Sierra catalogue, and when were they each active?"
